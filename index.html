Hello, world! I am Misha

A front-end developer based in the Czech republic.


I have passion for clean elegant design, UI effects and dynamic user experiences. With high attention to detail I enjoy solving complex problems, turning visual and technical aspects together and bringing visions alive.


At all I help individuals and small bussineses to be better online.


And what is my developer story?

It begun when first of my two incredible sons born. Then I got an opportunity in online marketing. And while I was improving my SEO skills a got close to my old dream. To web development. So I tried to learn HTML and now I am on my developer and designer journey.
